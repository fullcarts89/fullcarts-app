How It Works

Built on community intelligence.

Every data point comes from real shoppers. The community submits, the community validates, and the data stays open for everyone.

1

Scan or Search

Point your camera at any grocery barcode. FullCarts instantly checks if that product is in the database and shows you its full shrink history.

2

Report What You Find

Spotted a size reduction? Submit a report in under a minute. Paste a Reddit post URL and our parser auto-fills the form. Earn XP for every verified submission.

3

Community Validates

Other shoppers confirm reports with an "I've seen this too" tap. Three independent confirmations earns a Verified badge. No single person controls the data.

Our Mission

One rule: we're on the shopper's side.

We exist to make the invisible visible. Every shrink event we document is one more data point that makes it harder for companies to quietly take from shoppers. The data is public, the process is open, and the community controls it.

Open the App

No cost to shoppers. No ads, no premium tiers, no paywalls. The app and all its data are free to use.

Open data. Every submission is anonymized and publicly downloadable as a CSV. No sign-up required.

Community validated. Reports require multiple independent confirmations before earning a Verified badge.

Transparent about money. We work with advocacy orgs, researchers, and journalists who want to use the data to hold companies accountable — for a fee that covers operating costs only.

No deals that compromise the data. We don't work with companies that have a financial interest in what this data says.

Who We Work With

Amplifying voices on the shopper's side.

We're building toward an ecosystem where consumer-aligned organizations get the tools they need, and brands with a verified track record of transparency get recognized for it.
