## What changed since June 8

One decision reshapes everything: **you are now the face of FullCarts.** The June 8 plan argued "faceless = hands + product + voiceover." The research that informed it actually undercut that thesis, and a month of newer data makes the reversal clear-cut:

- **Opening on a talking head beats opening on b-roll by ~10% on 3-second retention** — and 50–60% of all drop-off happens in those first 3 seconds. Face-forward isn't a vanity choice; it's a retention choice. ([OpusClip](https://www.opus.pro/blog/youtube-shorts-hook-formulas))
- **Face-on-camera lifts thumbnail/preview click-through 25–30%** and builds the parasocial trust that an *authority* brand lives or dies on. ([virvid.ai](https://virvid.ai/blog/faceless-vs-face-camera-youtube-growth-monetization-engagement-2026))
- **Founder-led / expert-authority content out-engages faceless brand content by 5–10×**, and 73% of decision-makers say thought leadership is a more trustworthy signal of capability than marketing (Edelman 2025). ([HubSpot](https://www.hubspot.com/startups/sales-and-marketing/founder-led-content-strategy))
- The finfluencer space is **flooded with fake authority** (FINRA flagged this in 2025: reach without real expertise). You have the opposite problem solved for you — **verifiable** authority that competitors literally cannot copy.

**What does *not* change:** the database is still the backstage moat (infinite sourced stories, every claim with a BLS/USDA/receipt trail), real evidence still does the proving (AI may decorate, never testify — see the three buckets), and automation is still limited to *ideation* (the content-brief generator), never finished production.

## Positioning: the category-of-one

There is no named, face-forward, credentialed shrinkflation watchdog with a database. **That role is unclaimed — so claim it.**

> **"Rossen Reports for shrinkflation, but with a 3,000-product receipts database and a data-scientist's eye."**

Jeff Rossen (@rossen.reports, 2.4M+, ex-NBC consumer investigator) is the role model, **not** the existing faceless shrinkflation creators (Neal Chauhan, Melissa Simonson). The pattern every winning authority creator shares — Rossen, Vivian Tu ("Your Rich BFF", ex-Wall St), Humphrey Yang, Tori Dunlap — is **face on camera + a clearly stated credential/mission + a recurring named segment.** The mission *is* the brand.

### Your two credibility hooks (and how to use them)

You sit on a rare combination. Use both, but differently:

**1. The database — lead with this, openly, always.**
> "I run the largest public shrinkflation database — 3,000+ products, all backed by government data."

This is your primary, unrestricted credential. Put the receipts on screen; the dataset does the flexing so you don't have to. It is self-evidently real, which is exactly what audiences reward in a fake-authority-saturated niche.

**2. The day-job — insinuate, sparingly, as a signature.**
You don't want to name your employer (smart — it caps employer-policy and personal-brand risk). Use the *insinuation* you proposed, as a recurring signature credential rather than a constant drumbeat:
> "I do data for a company billions of people on this planet have used — so reading numbers for a living is literally my job. Here's the trick I spotted."

**Recommended frequency: roughly 1 in 4–5 videos**, plus your bio/pinned post. Reasoning:
- **Always-on in the bio** ("Data person by day. I build the receipts on shrinkflation.") — sets the frame for every new visitor with zero per-video cost.
- **Sparingly in-video** keeps it a *signature*, not a tic. Overusing an authority flex reads as insecure and invites "if you're so smart, why do you keep saying it." Used 1-in-5, it lands as confident context.
- **Always in service of the viewer**, never as a status flex: frame it as *"I have a skill I'm using to protect you,"* not *"I'm smarter than you."* ("I read numbers for a living, and the way they hid this size cut is genuinely clever — let me show you.")
- **Pair authority with humility.** The 2025 winning combo is expertise + vulnerability: "I almost missed this one myself."

> **Add a standing disclaimer** in bio and on any video that leans on the day-job: *"Personal project. Views my own."* Cheap insurance for the employer-brand boundary.

## Format: hybrid (your pick, and the right one)

You film **hybrid**: your face carries the hook and the key human moments; hands-on-product and screen-recorded data carry the proof. This is the highest-leverage format for you because it satisfies both the retention data (open on a face) and the evidence policy (the proof layer is real footage + real screenshots, never AI).

**The anatomy of a FullCarts hybrid clip (30–60s):**

| Beat | On screen | Job |
|---|---|---|
| **Hook (0–3s)** | **Your face**, talking, + a bold text overlay with a number | Stop the scroll. Open on you, not b-roll. |
| **Proof (3–15s)** | Hands on the real product + the FullCarts entry / BLS chart screenshot | Make the number believable. This is the moat. |
| **Context (15–40s)** | You (voiceover or to-camera) + Remotion data-viz overlay pinned to the product | Explain the trick; drop your credibility signature here when used. |
| **Payoff (40–55s)** | The kicker — the most outrageous comparison, restated | Evoke the reaction that drives comments/shares. |
| **CTA (final 3–5s)** | You + text overlay | "Follow — I find these so you don't get got. Search any product on fullcarts.org." |

Full scripting template and 5 worked examples live in `docs/content/first-batch.md`.

## Content mix (authority-driven blend)

Replaces the June 8 pillar split. Tuned for a credentialed data creator: educational core, newsjacking as the reach engine, personal story for trust.

| Bucket | Share | What it is for FullCarts | Home |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Educational / evergreen explainer** (authority core) | **~40%** | "How the 14% shrink trick works," database deep-dives, methodology, "what 3,000 products taught me" | TikTok/Reels/Shorts |
| **Reactive / newsjacking** (reach engine) | **~25%** | New BLS/CPI print, USDA outlook, a brand caught this week, a viral receipt — react *with the database* same-day | X-led, clip to TikTok |
| **Personal / behind-the-scenes** (parasocial trust) | **~15%** | Why you built the database, "data person by day," the product that personally set you off | TikTok/Reels |
| **Entertainment / hook-driven** (top-of-funnel) | **~15%** | "Guess how much smaller this got," satisfying reveals, rankings, rage-but-true | TikTok/Reels |
| **Promotional / CTA** (convert) | **~5%** | "Search any product on FullCarts," newsletter | all |

**Hold the 90/10 trust rule: ~90% value, ≤10% promotion.** The authority you bank is what makes the rare ask land.

**Why newsjacking is the secret weapon:** government data drops are *scheduled, recurring* newsjack opportunities. BLS CPI lands monthly; USDA food-price outlooks on a calendar. Everyone else reacts with opinions — you react with a verifiable database, same day. Build the editorial calendar around these dates (the brief generator already ingests `fred_cpi_data` / `bls_shrinkflation`).

**Account-safety bonus:** 3–5 hand-made clips/week is also the safe new-account ramp — it won't trip spam heuristics.

## Growth mechanics (absorbed from the 0→1M playbook)

A creator-growth playbook maps cleanly onto our build. We adopt its *principles* at *our*
sustainable tempo (it's built for daily speedrunning; we're authority-building beside a day job —
and it explicitly blesses "can't post daily → run a soft series").

1. **Bingeability via a signature series** — our biggest former gap, now fixed: the **"Caught:"**
   soft series is the spine, with "How They Hid It" / "Report Card" / "Same Price, Less Stuff" as
   future threads. Connective tissue (identical cold-open + end-screen teases) turns one view into
   many → the follow. See `docs/content/series.md`.
2. **Signature visual style** (look · set · framing · editing) — define it once so every video is
   instantly "ours." Cheapest high-leverage lever = a recognizable **set** (e.g. a product "shrink
   wall" or a clean data-desk). Our **Remotion overlays are already the signature editing element**;
   pair with a fixed set + consistent eye-level framing. *(Set build = a near-term to-do.)*
3. **Unique edge** — we're elite here: the database is genuinely *new information* nobody else has.
   Keep leading with "I have the receipts + the government data."
4. **Hooks** — beyond the verbal Hook System, add: **(a) a visual hook** (open on the product/real
   chart — scroll-stopping), **(b) cult-hopping** (lead with the recognizable brand/logo for instant
   relatability — free for us), **(c) speed-to-value** (signal value by ~2.5s / the second sentence),
   and **(d) The Snap** 3-beat structure. All in `hooks.md`.
5. **Storytelling archetype** — pick the one that plays to our strengths: breakdown / case-study /
   by-the-numbers, with personal-narrative on origin pieces (the current meta).
6. **Posting & iteration** — keep **3–5×/week** (not daily). Adopt the discipline: review every
   **~10 posts**, sort by **follows-driven** (not views), and double down on the outlier. Chase
   **ignition** via a **super-share** (a big account / journalist reshare — our X + reporter angle)
   and aim to clear **~5K followers** (the "minimum-viable-credibility" line) as fast as possible.



Every single post must satisfy **all five non-negotiables**:

1. **Data-driven** — anchored to a specific FullCarts datapoint *or* a credible macro event (BLS/CPI/USDA/FRED). A number is always on screen.
2. **Interesting** — a real hook, a curiosity loop, a surprise. Never a dry stat read.
3. **Credible** — framed from authority: the database (always) and/or the day-job signature (sparingly), in service of the viewer.
4. **Relatable** — speaks to someone who doesn't want to get ripped off at the store. "You," not "consumers."
5. **Reaction-evoking** — a kicker, a "wait, what?", a question — something that pulls a comment/share.

If a draft misses any one, it doesn't ship. The brief generator scores candidates against these; the pre-publish checklist enforces them.

## Carried forward from June 8 (unchanged, still authoritative)

- **The content-brief generator** (`pipeline/scripts/generate_content_briefs.py`, to build): convergence detector — FullCarts signal × external convergence (news/macro/trend/calendar) → ranked, evidence-backed idea digest. Now also scores each candidate against the 5 non-negotiables. Still ideation-only; it never writes finished posts.
- **AI vs. real-evidence policy (the three buckets):** MUST be real (anything carrying a claim — product, charts, dashboard, headlines), CAN be AI (connective/atmosphere, labeled), NEVER AI (fake packaging, fabricated charts/dashboards). The one-question test: *"Could a viewer mistake this for evidence of a fact, product, or number?"* Yes → real. This is non-negotiable and the reason two launch videos got flagged. With a face-forward format, **you are now the primary real-evidence asset** — your authentic presence is the hardest thing to fake.
- **Reusable real-asset kit:** screen-recorded FullCarts pages, screenshotted source charts (ICE coffee, FRED CPI), a small product b-roll bank. Makes "real" the fast path.

## Gaps and risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| On-camera production is heavier than faceless | Hybrid format + batch filming once/week; Captions app removes most edit labor; reuse asset kit |
| Employer-brand / policy exposure from the day-job hook | Never name the employer; insinuate only; ~1-in-5 frequency; standing "views my own" disclaimer |
| Authority reads as arrogant | Lead with the receipt not the résumé; pair with vulnerability; frame as "a skill I use to protect you" |
| Burnout at 4–6 hrs/week | The whole production playbook is engineered around one batch day + AI-assisted edit; quiet weeks are fine at 3 clips |
| AI visuals trip synthetic-media filters | Three-bucket policy held; new account stays AI-free until trusted; your face is real-evidence |
| Brand-callout read as harassment | Neutral documentary framing; on-screen source citations; factual data only; de-emphasize logos |
| Newsjack window missed | Pre-plan the CPI/USDA calendar; brief generator fires an event-triggered alert on a tracked-brand news break or CPI print |
